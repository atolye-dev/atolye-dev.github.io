Privacy Policy

English

This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku application handles user information.

The application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with external servers or third parties.

All game-related data such as Sudoku progress, completed games, statistics, and preferences are stored only locally on the user's device using a local database.

The application does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, or cloud storage.

Users can remove their locally stored data by uninstalling the application or clearing the application's storage from device settings.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
